Pull&Bear

Spain

Pull&Bear (Spanish: [pul am ˈbeɾ]) is a Spanish clothing and accessories retailer based in Narón (A Coruña), Galicia. It was founded in 1986 as New Wear, S.A. and it was officially launched as Pull & Bear in 1991. It is part of Inditex, 4 owner of Zara and Bershka brands. The name came from the word "pull" like "pull from the shelf" and "bear" like "put it on".
