Tom Misch

Thomas Abraham Misch (born 25 June 1995) is an English musician and producer. He began releasing music on SoundCloud in 2012[7] and released his debut studio album Geography in 2018.[8] In 2020, he released his second studio album What Kinda Music in collaboration with English jazz drummer Yussef Dayes,[9] distributed through Blue Note Records.
